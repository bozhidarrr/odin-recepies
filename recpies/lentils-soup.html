Lentil soup

Ingredients

  • lentils - 500gr
  • red bell papper - 1
  • onions - 1
  • garlic - 1 bulb
  • tomatoes - 2
  • carrot - 1
  • herbs - to taste

Steps

  1. Finely dice all vegatables
  2. In a pre-heated pot brown the onions, garlic and the carrot
  3. Add the pre-washed lentils and enough water to cover it
  4. Add herbs to your liking
  5. Cook until the lentils is done
  6. Add the finelly chopped tomatoes
  7. Add salt
  8. Cook for 5 more minutes
  9. Take off the stove
  10. Serve with fresh parsley
